--- App Review Information (App Store Connect > App Information > Review Notes) ---

Review Notes (paste into App Store Connect):
"
- LINE and Google login are displayed as "Coming Soon" and are intentionally
  non-functional in this version. Apple Sign-In is the only active login method.
  LINE and Google OAuth will be activated in a future update.
- The app uses a monthly auto-renewable subscription (500 JPY/month).
  A "Restore Purchases" button is available on the paywall screen.
- UGC report and block features are available via the 3-dot menu on any post.
  Reports are stored locally and will be synced to our moderation backend
  in a future server update.
- Account deletion is available at: Profile > Account Deletion.
  All local data (pins, saved spots, profile, settings) is permanently removed.
- The app does NOT use Google Maps. It uses OpenStreetMap via flutter_map (OSM).
  No Google Maps API key is present in the build.
"

--- Privacy Nutrition Labels (App Store Connect > App Privacy) ---

Configure these in App Store Connect exactly:

1. Location (Approximate/Precise)
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: No
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: Display nearby spots on map, route guidance, pin location for posts

2. Photos or Videos
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: Yes (photos attached to user-generated pins)
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: Users attach photos to spot pins they create

3. User ID (Apple User Identifier)
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: Yes
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: Authentication via Sign in with Apple

4. Email Address
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: Yes
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: Provided optionally via Sign in with Apple for account recovery

5. Name (Display Name)
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: Yes
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: Shown on user profile, provided via Sign in with Apple

6. Purchases
   - Usage: App Functionality
   - Linked to User: Yes
   - Tracking: No
   - Purpose: In-app subscription status tracking
